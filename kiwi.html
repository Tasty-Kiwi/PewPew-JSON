How to convert markdown files into HTML:

You don't need to have lowdown or markdown.pl to convert your precious markdown files into HTML. You can simply do that with PowerShell one-liner:
(ConvertFrom-Markdown .\input.md).Html | Out-File -Encoding utf8 .\output.html
